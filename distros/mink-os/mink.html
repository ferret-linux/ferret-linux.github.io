Fig. 01 — Overview

Bare ground.
No noise.
Just system.

Mink-OS is a TTY-only, headless-first immutable Linux. No desktop. No bloat. Non-free codecs built in, out-of-tree kmods included, ZFS and v4l2loopback ready. A clean base for custom images and headless environments.

Fast by design.

Norix-OS is a Wayland-native, performance-first immutable Linux. Niri WM paired with Noctalia shell — preconfigured, opinionated, and fully customizable. Ghostty as your terminal. No flatpak store preloaded. Just a fast, clean base ready for whatever you throw at it.
